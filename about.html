About Us

Make impossible things happen

TEQFEST Teqfest is an annual event organized in BCET campus which provides platform for students to come up with their creative and innovative ideas in a competitive environment. It provides them an opportunity to showcase their skills in front of big crowd. Teqfest is entirely managed by students of BCET GURDASPUR. There are a number of committees who overlook all the aspects activities of Teqfest. Each event has its own Coordinator and Teacher incharge as well for fare conduct of all events. Over the past years, Teqfest helped the students to develop their focus on current Technical and Social Issues. In its endeavour to provide an international platform to the youth to showcase their talents and skills set in fierce competitions, display cutting edge technology and research from all over globe, showcase a perfect blend of entertainment and technology. Over the years Teqfest has gradually diversified into an organization that apart from showcasing the pinnacle of technical advancement strives for betterment of the society by developing an inclination of youth towards the current social issues through unique initiatives. Be in campaign focused at promotion of futuristic and eco-friendly technologies or technical innovation aimed at a progressive and sustainable INDIA, these initiatives to address social and environmental causes have set Teqfest apart from any other college festival and added a whole new dimension to this organization working on non-profit basis.
